Settings

Ship tracking uses aisstream.io. Sign up (free, ~30 seconds), copy your key, and paste it here. The key is stored only in your browser (localStorage) and is only used to connect to aisstream directly.

Runs a 15 s subscription to Singapore Strait (guaranteed-busy shipping lane) using your key. Proves whether the key works independent of the water around your current map center.

If you see messages flowing but no ship markers, the subscribed bounding box may not match your map center — pan to a busy port (e.g. Rotterdam, Singapore, Houston) and wait ~30 s. aisstream's free tier only streams the bbox you subscribed to.

Copies a compact JSON snapshot of your current session (center, range, selected contact + sampled track, AIS counters) to the clipboard. Paste into a GitHub issue when reporting a bug so the exact state is captured. Privacy: your user agent is not included; map center and AIS bounding box are rounded to ~370 ft so your exact location is not exposed; your aisstream key is never included.
